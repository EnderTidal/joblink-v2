Let's get your office set up — about 5 minutes.

What JobLink does

JobLink fills job orders with self-selected, interested candidates. You'll do everything through Tom — a chat with four buttons: create job orders, send Magic Blasts (a text with each candidate's personal job link), review results, and get help.

This setup walks you through two things: securing your account, and connecting Whippy so blasts can actually send texts. You can skip Whippy for now and stay in safe practice mode.

Secure your account

You're currently on the default password everyone can read in the docs. Pick your own (8+ characters).

Connect Whippy (texting)

Whippy is the platform that actually sends your texts. JobLink needs three things from your Whippy account. Log into Whippy in another tab and follow along.

1 — Get your API Key

  1. In Whippy, click Settings (gear icon in the left sidebar)
  2. Scroll down the settings menu to Developers (near the bottom)
  3. Open Developers — you'll see the API keys tab
  4. Click + Add key, name it JobLink, then click the copy icon
Settings sidebar — scroll down to find Developers
Developers → API keys — click the copy icon to grab your key

2 — Get your From Number

  1. Go to Settings → Channels
  2. Find your SMS channel — the phone number in the Sender column is your From Number
Settings → Channels — your From Number is in the Sender column

3 — Get your Channel ID

  1. Click the three dots (⋮) next to your channel → Edit Channel
  2. Copy the full Place ID value from the channel detail page
Click the three dots (⋮) next to your channel to open details
Channel detail page — the Channel ID is the Place ID field
Not ready? That's fine — skip this step and JobLink stays in practice mode: everything works, but no real texts can be sent. You can connect Whippy any time in Admin → Settings.
